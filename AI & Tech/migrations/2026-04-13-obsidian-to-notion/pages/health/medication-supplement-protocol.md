# Daily Medication & Supplement Protocol

Built around Adderall IR 10mg 3x daily. ~~Oxcarbazepine 50mg 3x daily~~ **[DISCONTINUED — as of 2026]**

---

## Morning — 8:00 AM

| What | Dose | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Adderall IR | 10mg | First dose |
| Propranolol | 10mg | Smooth stimulant peaks/valleys |
| L-Theanine (Razer Mints) | 150mg | Blunts cortisol spike from Adderall — keeps focus without jitters or anxiety |
| Multivitamin | As directed | General health |
| Probiotics | As directed | Gut health |
| Fish Oil | As directed | Anti-inflammatory, brain health |
| Creatine | As directed | Performance & cognition |
| Vitamin D3 & K2 | As directed | Immune & bone health |
| **Protein before dosing** | Shake/bar | Dopamine is built from amino acids — no protein = less raw material for focus |

> **Avoid Vitamin C in the morning.** Ascorbic acid acidifies urine and speeds up Adderall excretion — shortens your window and worsens the crash. Take it at night instead.

---

## Afternoon — 12:00 PM

| What | Dose | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Adderall IR | 10mg | Second dose |
| Propranolol | 10mg | Mood stabilization |

---

## Evening — 5:00 PM

| What | Dose | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Adderall IR | 10mg | Third dose |
| Propranolol | 10mg | Mood stabilization |

> Adderall IR has a 4–6 hour half-life. Your 5pm dose is active until 9–11pm. This is why winding down feels hard. The night stack below helps counter this.

---

## Night — Before Bed

| What | Dose | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Magnesium Glycinate | 200–400mg | Stimulants deplete magnesium. Magnesium produces GABA — the chemical that lets you wind down. No magnesium = no GABA = no real sleep. |
| Vitamin C | As directed | Moved from morning — at night it helps clear the medication before sleep instead of shortening your daytime window. |

> Avoid screens during comedown. When dopamine drops below baseline your brain becomes hypersensitive to stimulation. Scrolling spikes dopamine in small bursts and makes evenings harder to recover from.

---

## Notes

- All additions (L-Theanine, Magnesium Glycinate) should be confirmed with your prescriber. ~~Note: Oxcarbazepine affects electrolyte/sodium balance.~~ **[Oxcarbazepine DISCONTINUED — as of 2026]**
- L-Theanine is fine with morning dose only — no need to stack with 5pm dose.
- Hard cutoff time for last dose should be calculated based on desired sleep time minus 5–6 hours.
