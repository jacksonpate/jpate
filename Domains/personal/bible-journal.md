## 2/24/26 — One College Highlands: "The Love of God"

Jackson Pate — church notes.

> **1 John verse 19** — "We love because he first loved us."

Our generation has watered down love.

**3 Misunderstandings of love:**
- "Love is just a feeling" → If love is just a feeling, then I will have apathy towards God
- "Love = Approval" → If love is approval, then correction from God feels like rejection
- "Love is conditional" → If love is conditional, then shame will keep me away from God

Your ability to love God and others is directly connected to your willingness to receive the love of God for yourself.

If God's love changes everything — "How do we receive his love?"

"God is love"

**"Agape"** — Unconditional Love — 1 Corinthians 13:4-8

**How to receive God's love:**
1. Quit outsourcing God's love
2. Open your eyes to the miracle that God chose you
3. Walk in love
