# Journal — 11/27/25 — Thanksgiving

*"Sometimes I just feel so out of place even at my own family's Thanksgiving."* Struggling with seasonal depression. Felt out of his own life for what felt like forever. Couldn't remember the last time he felt like Jackson Pate. Not alone on paper but *"I am ALONE."* Emptiness deep in his chest. Drew a sad and happy face side by side. Wrote *"When will I be better? Will I ever be?"* and *"I'm about to lose my mind."*
