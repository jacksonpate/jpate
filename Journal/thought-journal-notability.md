## 9/27/25 — Saturday

I guess this will be the start of my own thoughts, experiences and ideas. This might be a journey but I feel as if I have needed this for a while. Talking to friends and family is great but sometimes you just need to talk to yourself. Because honestly who knows you more than you? No one. There is not a soul on this earth that knows you as much as yourself — you know the ups and downs, the good and the bad, the goodness in yourself and the hatred that you have in your heart.

I just think sometimes: who is Jackson, who am I? I mean obviously I know I'm myself, but how can I come to that conclusion if I don't know myself? I just feel like I have always been the person that everyone wants me to be. I gave myself a mask, but I eventually became the mask. So now who am I? Who is under the mask? Who is this mystery under the mask — or is there even anybody under the mask? Did I become the mask?

I don't actually believe I have zero idea who I am. I see pieces of me come out here and there, but how do I emerge? How do I know it will be better?

I felt myself come out last night for the first true time in awhile when I saw her, talked to her. She brought out the me I remember in high school. It just felt natural — not that I had to try to mask myself to be liked.

This — I'm saying it now — I want to find myself, my journey, my purpose. I'm ready. I am finally ready to wake up. I only have so much time to live, but by God's grace I will live MY life. Not a mask, not questioning myself, but my own God-given life. This life is too short to be living in misery and confusion. It is my choice how to live my life — either float through my life or take it over as mine.

Such as today: I felt bad because I did not really do anything, but I might have done the most important thing I will ever do. I realized — I realized that I was lost. I'm not depressed, but I am lost, and I really only want to find my way, to see the light. When will I know I'm ready? You want? It's a leap of faith.

I think I know why I feel unfulfilled. I am such a worldly person. I don't know, but I feel scared to get close with God and I don't know why. I don't know where to start. But I want to feel fulfilled. Should I isolate myself? Should I limit myself to only myself? No. I can do everything, I can be myself while I'm learning to become me.

> **"Self love is the cure to self hate"**

This quote hit me really hard. I really appreciate the message behind this. It shows that all of your insecurities, issues, anger — all will go away once you start loving yourself.
