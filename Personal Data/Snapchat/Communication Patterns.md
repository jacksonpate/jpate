It's either a secondary account Jackson maintains, someone else using a near-identical username, or an alt. The 1045 sent / 1174 received split looks realistic for two-way use. Worth investigating — if it's his own secondary, those snaps don't count as relationship data. If it's someone else, they are by far his most active snap contact and completely unidentified.

But the snap send ratio (Jackson receives 4.9× more than he sends) and the known tension context means this is a complex relationship. High structural presence, potentially complicated emotional reality.

**edenprice27: 169 messages in ~7 days (Aug 2021)** — Most concentrated burst in the dataset. No context provided, but this density suggests something significant happened in that week.
